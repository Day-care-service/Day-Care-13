Infants

Ages:  1 month to 11 months

Sweet laughter and soft lullabies are where imagination and learning begins.  Teachers in our infant classrooms are committed to creating a safe and nurturing environment where babies can explore and make new discoveries.  We use "Creative Curriculum for Infants and Toddlers" and we also do weekly assessments and observations.  These assessments and observations are recorded and twice a year parent/teacher conferences are scheduled so that the information collected can be shared.
 
Infants also have an outdoor area to explore and get fresh air.  In the summer and spring months infants enjoy short walks in our 6-passenger stroller.

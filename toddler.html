Toddlers


12 months-36 months

Curiosity and imagination are where learning begins.  In our Toddler classrooms, we turn play into meaningful learning experiences.  Teachers in these classrooms are committed to creating a safe and nurturing environment while being flexible and allowing the children to learn through play.

Children in our Toddler classes are introduced to many centers that include:

  • Art
  • Music
  • Blocks
  • Dramatic Play
  • Science
  • Fine Motor 
  • Gross Motor
  • Reading

​We use Creative Curriculum for Infants and Toddlers and also perform weekly assessments and observations.  Theses assessments and observations are recorded and shared with parents twice a year at our parent/teacher conferences.  Creative Curriculum directly aligns with our state's Early Learning Standards.

Welcome to ABC Daycare
A fun, safe, clean, nurturing, and educational environment for children ages infant to preschool.

ABC Daycare provides quality care for the children through child-centered learning programs where children are nurtured and respected.  We offer activities that cover a wide range of developmental areas, including physical, social, emotional, and cognitive daily.

The center promotes the development of the whole child through developmentally appropriate activities and curriculum.  Parents can be confident of the care their children receive as relationships are developed with our staff and other children in the center.

Highlights/Quick Facts:

  • Safe and Secure
  • Developmentally Appropriate Curriculum
  • Caring, Competent, Highly Certified, Educated and Compassionate Staff
  • Increased Parent Engagement/Communication
  • Nutritious Breakfast, Lunch and Snack Included
  • Accepting Childcare Subsidies
